BillReminder Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

BillReminder does not collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information (PII) on external servers. All bill data, payment records, and reminders are stored locally on your device.

2. Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Data is stored on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms.
  • iCloud Sync: If enabled, your data syncs through your personal iCloud account. We do not access or store your iCloud data.

3. Notifications

Local Notifications: Bill reminders are generated locally on your device. No data is transmitted to any server.

4. In-App Purchases

Subscriptions and purchases are processed securely through Apple StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment information.

5. Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data-collection services.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your information with any third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

BillReminder does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected in the revised date above.
