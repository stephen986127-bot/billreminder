Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions and select BillReminder.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchase".

How does iCloud sync work?

Data syncs automatically through your personal iCloud account if enabled.

How do I export my data?

Go to Stats and tap "Export CSV".
